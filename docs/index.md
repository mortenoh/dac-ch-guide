# DAC Guides

Welcome to the guides for the **DHIS2 Annual Conference (DAC)** and its **climate track**.

These guides walk you through the hands-on sessions step by step. Each one is short
(around 5-6 minutes of following along) and built to be done at your own pace during
the session.

## How these guides work

Every guide follows the same rhythm:

1. **Read the step.** Each section explains what you are about to do and why it matters.
2. **Do the assignment.** Look for the highlighted boxes - these are the parts you need
   to do yourself.
3. **Confirm it worked.** Before moving on, check that the result matches what the guide
   describes.

!!! note "Assignment"
    Assignment boxes look like this. When you see one, pause and complete the step before
    continuing. Don't move on until the check passes.

## Before you start

Make sure your environment is ready. The climate-track guides assume you have a working
DHIS2 instance and the supporting tooling configured.

!!! note "Assignment: confirm your setup"
    - [ ] DHIS2 is running and you can log in.
    - [ ] chap-core is configured and reachable.
    - [ ] You have the credentials and access you were given for the session.

    If any of these fail, flag it now - the later guides build on this foundation.
